Safety: Your Fortress of the Future

When it comes to keeping you safe, this smart home takes things seriously. It’s got everything from high-tech fire alarms to a squad of venomous snakes (yup, you heard that right). Safety isn’t just a feature here—it’s an art form. Let’s break down how this house has you covered, 24/7.

Fire Alarm: Warning Lights & Voice Alerts

We’ve all heard fire alarms, but this one doesn’t just beep at you—it talks to you. When things heat up, the house alerts you with a booming voice and flashing lights that make sure you know exactly what's going down. No more wondering if that beeping is just your toast burning or something more serious.
